ü§î Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I start tracking my moods?

Simply click "Record Your Mood ‚ú®" on the main dashboard, select one of the 16 emotions, set the intensity (1-5), optionally add a note or voice recording, and save. It takes less than 30 seconds!

Q: What's the difference between Free and Premium?

Free users get 5 mood entries per month and basic analytics. Premium ($4.99/month) unlocks unlimited everything, advanced AI insights, extended meditation library, custom moods, and priority support. Try the 7-day free trial!

Q: Is my mood data private and secure?

Absolutely! Your mood data is encrypted and only visible to you unless you explicitly choose to share with friends. We never sell personal data and follow strict privacy standards. Voice notes and photos are especially protected.

Q: Can family members use the same account?

Each person should have their own account for privacy and personalized insights. Our Family Plan ($12.99/month) supports up to 6 accounts with parental controls and family dashboard features.

Q: How accurate are the AI insights?

Our AI analyzes your personal patterns and becomes more accurate over time. With 2+ weeks of data, insights are typically 80-90% relevant. The more you track consistently, the better the predictions become!

Q: What if I'm in crisis or having suicidal thoughts?

Your safety is our top priority. MoodVerse automatically detects concerning language and provides immediate crisis resources including 24/7 hotlines. However, please don't wait - contact emergency services (911) or crisis helplines immediately if you're in danger.
